Last updated June 2023

What information do we collect?

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details and email address when you opt for a callback, or engage in any other interactions with us.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We will post the updated policy on our website and indicate the date of the last revision. We encourage you to review the policy periodically for any changes.

By choosing Sugarcoat for your beauty needs, you entrust us with your personal information, and we value that trust. Rest assured that we are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security to safeguard your information.
