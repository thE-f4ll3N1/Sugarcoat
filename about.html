ABOUT US

Welcome to Sugarcoat: Where Beauty Meets Sweetness!

At Sugarcoat, we believe that beauty should be a delightful experience that leaves you feeling pampered, confident, and ready to take on the world. Our unisex salon is dedicated to providing top-notch services for hair, skin, makeup, and nails, all under one roof. As passionate professionals in the industry, we have created a haven where everyone can indulge in the sweetness of self-care.

Behind the scenes of Sugarcoat you'll find a team of talented and courteous individuals who are experts in their respective fields. From hair stylists and colorists to celebrity makeup artists and nail technicians, our team is handpicked to ensure that you receive the highest quality service tailored to your unique needs and preferences.

Every aspect of our salon is thoughtfully designed to create a soothing and delightful atmosphere.

At Sugarcoat, we are dedicated to using only the highest quality products and techniques to ensure exceptional results.

Come and experience the sweetness

Sugarcoat is more than just a business to us. We are deeply committed to building a strong sense of community and fostering genuine connections with our clients. We value your trust and strive to create a safe and welcoming space where you can feel comfortable expressing your individuality. Whether you're looking for a complete makeover or simply a moment of relaxation, our team is here to provide the highest level of care and attention to ensure you leave feeling refreshed, revitalized, and, of course, sugarcoated.

Who owns Sugarcoat?

Sugarcoat is the brainchild of two visionary sisters who share a passion for beauty and a dedication to providing exceptional salon services. As the owners of Sugarcoat, they have poured their hearts and souls into creating a space where clients can indulge in a sweet-themed haven of self-care.

What services does Sugarcoat offer?

At Sugarcoat, we offer a wide range of services to cater to all your beauty needs. Our salon provides hair styling and coloring services, makeup application for various occasions, skincare treatments, and nail artistry. Whether you're looking for a trendy haircut, a flawless makeup look, a rejuvenating facial, or stunning nail designs, our talented staff is here to make you look and feel your best.

How qualified is the staff at Sugarcoat?

We take great pride in the expertise and professionalism of our team at Sugarcoat. Our staff consists of highly skilled professionals who have undergone rigorous training and possess extensive experience in their respective fields. From our hair stylists, beauticians and colorists to our celebrity makeup artists and nail technicians, each member of our team is committed to delivering exceptional results and ensuring your satisfaction.

What makes Sugarcoat a unique salon experience?

Sugarcoat stands out from other salons with its unique sweet-themed ambiance and attention to detail. Our salon is designed to provide a delightful and relaxing atmosphere where you can escape from the everyday hustle and indulge in self-care. We prioritize quality service and use only the finest products and techniques to ensure that you receive the best possible treatments. Our commitment to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment sets us apart, making Sugarcoat a truly exceptional salon experience.

Can I book an appointment at Sugarcoat?

Absolutely! We encourage you to schedule an appointment at Sugarcoat to secure your preferred time slot and ensure that you receive personalized attention from our expert team. You can easily book an appointment through our website or by giving us a call. Our friendly staff will be more than happy to assist you in finding the perfect date and time for your visit. We look forward to welcoming you to our sweet haven of beauty and relaxation.
